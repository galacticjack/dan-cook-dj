Bio

ABOUT DAN COOK

Dan has over 20 years experience working as a DJ. Originally from London; Dan worked in the music industry, managing licensing and distribution for all 4 of the major record labels as well as writing a monthly tastemaker column for legendary publication DJ Mag.

Whilst at the weekends he was consistently delivering upfront and eclectic selections as a DJ, Dan climbed the ranks to become a regular favourite at some of the UK's top venues and festivals such as Ministry of Sound, Pacha, Egg, The Cross, The Scala, The Lightbox, The Secret Garden Party and Glastonbury.

Currently working in A&R for Universal Music Group, Dan has been based in Bali for the past 10 years and has held DJ residencies at many of the island's key venues. Dan has also lent his expertise as a music consultant for Kilo Bali, Roosterfish, The Mirah Group and the Good Food Brotherhood.

Musician, music director, accomplished and versatile DJ... Dan has toured extensively across Europe and Asia and has been booked to warm up the crowd for headline acts such as Justice, Rudimental, LTJ Bukem, Pete Tong, Flight Facilities, Claptone, Timo Maas, Layo & Bushwaka, Sebastien Leger, Chase & Status, M.A.N.D.Y and countless others.

His background in House music, Techno, Electronica and Disco, combined with his love of Soul, Jazz, Funk, Hip Hop and World Music always results in unique, diverse DJ sets - everything from balearic sunset sessions and daytime pool parties to late night dancefloor action.
