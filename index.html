About

THE STORY

Originally from London, Dan has over 20 years experience as a DJ.

He worked in the music industry managing licensing and distribution for all 4 major record labels, while writing a monthly tastemaker column for legendary publication DJ Mag.

At weekends he climbed the ranks to become a favourite at the UK's top venues including Ministry of Sound, Pacha, Egg, The Cross, The Scala, The Lightbox, Secret Garden Party and Glastonbury.

Currently A&R at Universal Music Group, Dan has been based in Bali for 10 years. Also a music consultant for Kilo Bali, Roosterfish and The Mirah Group.

His background in House, Techno, Electronica and Disco, combined with love of Soul, Jazz, Funk, Hip Hop and World Music results in unique, diverse sets — from balearic sunset sessions to late night dancefloor action.
