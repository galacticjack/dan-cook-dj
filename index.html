Bio

ABOUT DAN COOK

Dan has over 20 years experience working as a DJ. Originally from London; Dan worked in the music industry, managing licensing and distribution for all 4 of the major record labels as well as writing a monthly tastemaker column for legendary publication DJ Mag.

Whilst at the weekends he was consistently delivering upfront and eclectic selections as a DJ, Dan climbed the ranks to become a regular favourite at some of the UK's top venues and festivals such as Ministry of Sound, Pacha, Egg, The Cross, The Scala, The Lightbox, The Secret Garden Party and Glastonbury.

Currently working in A&R for Universal Music Group, Dan has been based in Bali for the past 10 years and has held DJ residencies at many of the island's key venues.

His background in House music, Techno, Electronica and Disco, combined with his love of Soul, Jazz, Funk, Hip Hop and World Music always results in unique, diverse DJ sets.
